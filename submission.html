Authors

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original research papers not published or currently under review elsewhere. Submissions must be in English and follow the ACM double-column conference style.

Paper Format

All submissions must use the official LaTeX ACM article template (acmart.cls, v2.16+):

\documentclass[sigconf,authordraft]{acmart}

Templates: acm.org/publications/proceedings-template

Paper Length

Full Papers: Up to 10 pages (double-column, including figures, tables, and references). A clearly marked appendix is permitted.

Short Papers: Up to 5 pages (double-column, including all content).

Review Process

Double-blind review — author names, affiliations, and self-identifying references must be removed from submissions.

Minimum 3 reviews per paper. Acceptance rate capped at 40% per ICDCN steering committee policy.

Registration

ICDCN 2027 follows a single registration fee for the entire 4-day event. There is no workshop-only registration fee.

At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present in person.

Generative AI Policy

Use of generative AI tools is permitted in accordance with ACM guidelines.

Publication

Accepted papers will appear in the ACM ICPS companion proceedings, indexed by ACM, DBLP, and Scopus.

Authors not in the ACM Open program may be required to pay an APC. See ACM author guidance.
